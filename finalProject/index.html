Dogs


The dog family belongs to the group of meat-eating mammals called carnivores. Wild dogs include
Foxes, Jackals, Coyotes, and Wolves. Some wild dogs live and hunt on their own, but others,
such as wolves and African wild dogs, live and hunt together in packs. Domestic dogs, which are
often kept as pets or as working animals, are related to wolves.
